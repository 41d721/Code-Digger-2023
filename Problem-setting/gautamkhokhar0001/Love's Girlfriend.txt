								Love's Girlfriend
Love Bansal has just cleared JEE and he desperately wants a girlfriend in college, so will choose his college accordingly.

He decided to count the number of boys and the number of girls in the college
and saw that if the number of girls in college is strictly greater than half
of number of boys,then he might get a girlfriend else he will never get a  
girlfriend.

Let number of boys  be 'B' and number of girls in college be 'G',find out
whether love will get a girlfriend or not.

Input:
You are given t testcases.Each test case contains 2 integers B,G.

Output:
output on a single line "yes" if Love will get a girlfriend else "no".

Constraints:
1<=t<=1e7
B,G<=1e7.
